Who are we?

WICSE is a group focused on gathering together female students at University of South Florida who are majoring, or interested in, computer science and engineering. WICSE has currently between 60 and 70 female undergraduate and graduate students, who are currently majoring in Computer Science and Engineering at USF. WICSE provides opportunities for female students to participate in extracurricular activities, receive and provide support to fellow members regarding coursework, job opportunities, or general counseling advice, and create strong relationships between members, faculty, staff, and job recruiters.
